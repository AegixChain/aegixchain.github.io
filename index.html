// capabilities

Built for the threats
that don't exist yet.

AegixChain AI combines classical cybersecurity with modern machine learning — purpose-built for decentralized networks.

Cascade defense logic

Each failed defense layer automatically activates the next. No human intervention needed — the system escalates and responds autonomously.

Sub-millisecond response

Threats are detected and blocked in real time as transactions enter the mempool — before they are confirmed on-chain.

Cross-chain correlation

Track threat actors across Ethereum, BNB Chain, Polygon, and more — connecting the dots that single-chain monitors miss.

Behavioral baselining

AI models learn what "normal" looks like for your specific blockchain environment and flag statistical deviations in real time.

LLM-powered reports

Every incident generates a plain-English forensics report — what happened, which layer stopped it, and what the attacker was trying to achieve.

API-first architecture

Integrate AegixChain AI into your existing stack via REST API. Works with any blockchain node, wallet infrastructure, or DeFi protocol.
